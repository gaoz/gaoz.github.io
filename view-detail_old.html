Detail:

Discover the hidden beauty of paradise on this ultimate Oahu circle island tour! The island's most beautiful treasures await you on this unique Oahu eco tour, where you'll swim at a waterfall, see the famed big wave beaches and idyllic countryside of the North Shore, and even have lunch at a beautiful "secret beach"! On this 120-mile circle island tour of Oahu, you'll see offshore islands, breathtaking waterfalls, pristine beaches, walk through botanical gardens and lush tropical rainforest, and visit the best spots in between. A delicious and freshly made box picnic lunch is included with this tour and eaten at a secluded beach after having the chance to explore ancient Hawaiian fishponds. The highlight of the day would have to be a waterfall swim deep in the heart of Waimea Valley. Surrounded by verdant flora, you'll see an amazing diversity of plant life and learn interesting facts about Hawaii's delicate ecosystem. Another highlight is a stop to Laniakea Beach. Popularly referred to as "Turtle Beach" Laniakea is a Green Sea Turtle basking ground where these gentle ocean creatures come to sleep on the sand and swim in the shallows. Visit and experience lesser-known island locations like La'ie Point and the Byodo-In Temple. As you make your way back towards Waikiki, stop at the Nu'uanu Pali Lookout for the best view of Oahu's windward side, and see the rocky coast of the South Shore with fascinating natural landmarks like the Halona Blowhole and Hanauma Bay. Along the way you'll learn the fascinating secrets and history of Oahu, and also about the island's unique ecosystem from your local expert naturalist tour guide. On a small group tour like this with up to 24 passengers, you'll a more personalized experience with interaction from your guide, spend less time getting on and off the bus and have a more relaxing day with a better sightseeing rhythm.

Info:

Turtle sightings are very common but cannot be guaranteed since they are wild animals. Special Instructions Learn all bout Hawaii's legends, history, ecology and geology from your naturalist tour guide along the way! If you're looking for a little more adventure, check out the "Hidden Adventures of Hawaii" Tour in the Related Products tab Definitely bring a swimsuit & towel. A backpack with some light snacks are a great thing to bring, and don't forget the sunscreen. Most importantly don't forget your camera! A circle island tour is a great first activity to do on vacation because you'll see the whole island and have the chance to discover new places to return to.

Inclusions

Roundtrip Waikiki hotel transportation, picnic box lunch eaten at scenic beach location, admission to Waimea Valley Park, admission to Byodo-In Temple.
